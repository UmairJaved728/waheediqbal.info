Waheed Iqbal

Welcome! I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science program at PUCIT, University of the Punjab, Lahore Pakistan. I have completed my Ph.D., from the Asian Institute of Technology, master of engineering in computer science from the Asian Institute of Technology, and master of information technology from the Barcelona School of Informatics, Spain. I have contributed to OpenNebula as part of Google Summer of Code 2010.
